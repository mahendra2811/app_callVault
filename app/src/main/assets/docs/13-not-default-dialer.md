# callNest is not your default dialer

callNest deliberately stays out of the default-dialer role. We never replace your phone's call screen, and we never intercept dialling.

## Why it matters

Default-dialer apps gain extra privileges — including reading SMS, blocking calls system-wide, and full call control. They also incur extra Play Store policies and review delays. callNest does not need any of that.

## What this means for you

- **Outgoing calls** still launch your stock dialer. callNest just records the call once it ends.
- **Incoming calls** ring through the stock dialer. callNest's floating bubble overlays it instead.
- **Block list**: you can mark numbers as _Blocked_ inside callNest, but the OS-level block list is still managed by your phone's dialer or messaging app. We surface the OS block status as a tag for clarity.

## Switching back from another app

If you accidentally set a different dialer, open **Settings → Apps → Default apps → Phone app** and pick the one you want. callNest will keep working regardless of which dialer is active.

## Privacy

Because we are not a dialer, we cannot read or send SMS, cannot place calls on your behalf, and cannot mute your microphone.
