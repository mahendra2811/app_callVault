# Privacy

CallVault was built local-first. Your call data, contacts, notes, tags, and lead scores never leave your device unless you explicitly export them.

## What stays on this device
- The full call log (read from the OS).
- Your contact metadata (read from the OS contact provider).
- Notes, tags, follow-ups, bookmarks, filter presets, search history.
- Lead-score weights and computed scores.
- Backup archives — encrypted with your passphrase, written to your Documents folder.

## What we do not do
- We do not collect analytics. There is no telemetry SDK in the build.
- We do not sync to a cloud. CallVault has no server.
- We do not read your SMS, contacts you have not opened, or any account data outside the three permissions you explicitly grant.
- We do not place outbound calls or send messages.

## What you control
- **Clear search history** — wipes the local search log immediately.
- **Clear all notes** — removes every note and its history.
- **Reset all data** — wipes everything CallVault has stored on this device. Your OS call log and OS contacts are not touched.

## Diagnostics
Crash reports stay on-device. If you want to share one with support, use the **Send report** button on the about screen — it generates an attachment for an email you compose, so you choose what gets sent.
