# Getting started with callNest

callNest turns your phone's call log into a working sales and service notebook. Every incoming and outgoing number becomes a contact you can tag, take notes on, and follow up with — without ever leaving the app.

## First five minutes

1. Grant the three call-log, contacts, and phone-state permissions when asked. callNest cannot read your history without them.
2. Wait for the first sync to finish. The Calls tab shows a small refresh indicator at the top while it works.
3. Tap any call to open its detail screen. From there you can add notes, apply tags, set a follow-up reminder, or auto-save the number to your contacts.

## What you'll do every day

- **Calls tab**: triage today's activity, swipe right to bookmark, swipe left to archive.
- **Inquiries tab**: see numbers callNest auto-saved for you — typically unsaved callers from the last seven days.
- **My Contacts tab**: filter your saved contacts by tag, last-call date, or lead score.
- **Bookmarks**: pin people you want to revisit before tomorrow.

callNest never sends your data to a server. Everything lives on this device.
