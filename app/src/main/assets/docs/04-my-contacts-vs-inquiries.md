# My Contacts vs. Inquiries

Both tabs show real contacts on your device, but they answer different questions.

## My Contacts

Your normal phone contacts — anyone you, or a previous app, manually saved. callNest enriches them with tags, notes, lead score, and last-contact info, but it never modifies the underlying contact unless you ask.

Use this tab for proactive outreach: filter by tag, by last-call date, or by lead-score bucket and start dialling.

## Inquiries

Numbers callNest _auto-saved_ for you. Typically unsaved callers from the last week who reached you twice or more. They live in their own contact group so you can spot them at a glance.

## Why two tabs?

The split keeps your contact book clean. Sales-style discovery happens in Inquiries; long-term relationships live in My Contacts. Once you confirm an inquiry is worth keeping, tap **Promote to contact** in the detail screen — it moves out of the Inquiries group and into the rest of your address book.

## Convert and merge

If a number already exists under a different name, callNest flags a possible duplicate. Tap **Merge** to fold both records into one without losing notes or tags.
